# Privacy Policy for eMAG Promoted Remover

**Last Updated: May 1, 2026**

eMAG Promoted Remover ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our Chrome extension.

### 1. Data Collection
We do **not** collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).
We do **not** track your browsing history or user activity across websites other than the specific functionality described below.

### 2. Local Storage
The extension uses the `chrome.storage` API to store a single piece of data: the total count of promoted items removed. This data stays on your local device and is never sent to us or any third party.

### 3. Website Content
The extension reads the content of `emag.ro`, `emag.bg`, and `emag.hu` specifically to identify and remove sponsored product listings. This processing happens entirely within your browser; no website content is ever transmitted to external servers.

### 4. Third-Party Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties.

### 5. Contact
If you have questions, please contact us via the GitHub repository where this extension is hosted.
